Spring framework
----------------
This framework is used to build enterprise aplication such as web application,distributed application,web apis,etc
It is lightweight framework
It has upto 20 modules.Few modules are as follows
1)JDBC
2)AOP(Aspect orineted programming)
3)MVC(Model-view-controller)
4)ORM(Object relational mapping
5)Test
6)Context
7)Core
8)OXM(Object XML mapping)
etc
Every module is available in the form of dependency(one of more than one dependency)
